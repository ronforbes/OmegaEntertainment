Omega Entertainment is on a mission.

We're on a mission to bring the world together through the most universally enjoyed entertainment experiences. We were established in 2013 based on the premise that the most memorable games are the ones enjoyed with others. Our debut title, Block Party, is being enjoyed by hundreds of people around the world.

Block Party is our first game.

Block Party is a fast-paced, frantic puzzle game that blends approachable gameplay with hard-to-master tactics and strategy. An assortment of blocks, each with their own unique design and personality, must be matched to make them pop. Create killer combos by matching larger sets of blocks together. Pull off mighty chains by matching blocks that lead to more matches. But be quick about it; if the blocks reach the top of the board, it's game over!
